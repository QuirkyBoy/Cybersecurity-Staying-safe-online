Staying safe online

Staying safe online is important because it can help you protect yourself from cyber threats and scams. Here are some tips to help you stay safe online:

  • Be cautious when clicking on links or opening files from unknown sources
  • Use strong passwords and avoid using the same password for multiple accounts
  • Be careful what you post online
  • Don't give out personal information
  • Use secure VPNs and firewalls.
