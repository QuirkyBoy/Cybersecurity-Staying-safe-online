Viruses

Viruses are malicious software that attach to your computer and spread through your OS. They can be very easy do download accidentally, and there are many ways this could happen.

Where viruses can be obtained without consent (ways your PC can get infected)

  • Public charging stations
  • Dodgy USB sticks from online shopping
  • Dodgy links on dodgy websites

How to avoid getting infected

  • Don't click on links from unknown sources
  • Don't click on suspicious files
  • Don't open suspicious emails
  • Have virus protectors or anti-malware software installed

Don't be dumb and click on dodgy things or use dodgy apparatus
